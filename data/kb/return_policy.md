# Return Policy

## RETURN-UNOPENED: Unopened Items

Unopened physical products can be returned within 30 days of delivery when the customer can provide the order number and the item is not marked final sale.

## RETURN-OPENED: Opened Items

Opened items can be returned within 30 days only when they arrived damaged, defective, or materially different from the product description. A support manager must review opened-item returns above the automatic refund limit.

## RETURN-FINAL-SALE: Final Sale Items

Final sale and clearance items are not automatically eligible for refund. A support manager may review exceptions for damage, safety issues, or fulfillment mistakes.
