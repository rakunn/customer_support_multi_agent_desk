# Refund Policy

## REFUND-AUTO-LIMIT: Automatic Refund Limit

Refunds at or below 50 USD may be prepared automatically when the order is delivered, within the 30-day return window, not final sale, and the customer has no risk flags.

## REFUND-APPROVAL: Manager Approval Required

Human approval is required when the refund amount is above 50 USD, the item is final sale, the order is outside the return window, the customer has frequent refunds, or the request includes chargeback, fraud, legal, or abusive language.

## REFUND-PROMISES: Refund Promise Rule

Agents must not say a refund has been issued until the refund tool confirms that the approved mock refund was processed.
