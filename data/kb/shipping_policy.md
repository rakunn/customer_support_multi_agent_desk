# Shipping Policy

## SHIP-DOMESTIC: Domestic Shipping

Standard domestic shipping usually takes 3 to 5 business days after fulfillment. Support should create a follow-up ticket when tracking has not moved for more than 5 business days.

## SHIP-INTERNATIONAL: International Shipping

International shipping is available to selected countries. Delivery time depends on destination, customs processing, and local carriers.

## SHIP-ADDRESS: Address Changes

Customers can request an address change before the order ships. Once a package is in transit, support can create a carrier intercept ticket when the carrier supports it.
